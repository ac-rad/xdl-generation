To a stirred solution of ethyl propiolate (8.7g, 88.8mmol) was added bromine (5ml, 97mmol) dropwise over 10mins. The reaction was heated to reflux and stirred for 50mins then concentrated in vacuo. The orange residue was filtered through silica eluting with 20:1 petrol/ether to give the product as a pale yellow oil.
yield 22.82g, 99.7%
