A solution of alpha-cyclopropylbenzyl alcohol (3.6 g; 0.24 moles) in ether (10 cm3) was added to a stirred solution of magnesium bromide etherate (8.5 g; 0.33 mol) in ether (240 cm3) under an atmosphere of nitrogen.  The mixture was refluxed for 2.5 hours, then stirred overnight.
The mixture was washed with water (twice) and brine, then dried (MgSO4) and concentrated.
