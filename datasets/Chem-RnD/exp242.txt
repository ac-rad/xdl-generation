1,3-Dithiolium hexafluorophosphate (10 g, 0.040 mol) was dissolved in dry MeCN (200 ml) under argon and filtered into 1l round bottom flask with an argon inlet. Dry TEA was added dropwise (approx. 10 min.) and the solution was stirred for 15 min. Water (800 ml) was added slowly and the resulting mixture was stirred for another 15 min. Fine orange crystalline needles of product were filtered, sucked dry under a stream of nitrogen and stored in vacuum desiccator. Yield 3.95 g (97%).
