To a solution of 1.39 g (10.0 mmol) 1-(1,5-dimethyl-1H-1,2,3-triazol-4-yl)ethanone in 6 ml ethanol 0.69 g (10.0 mmol) hydroxylamine hydrochloride and 1.4 ml (10.0 mmol) triethylamine was added. Solution is warmed to boiling, volatiles were evaporated in vacuum. Residue was crystallized from water with charcoal to give1.23 g (80%) 1-(1,5-dimethyl-1H-1,2,3-triazol-4-yl)ethanone oxime.
